How to Be Human

Presentation Assignment

Sovereignty, identity, power of story

When I got this assignment, I thought it was just another boring and dreadful presentation assignment, but when I started working on it and listening to other people’s presentations, I realized that it was much more than that. Even though this assignment is not directly related to Indigenous and First Nations, the topic and theme me and my peers explore are interconnected with the ideas in this course, such as family, resilience, culture, struggles, and sovereignty. As someone who chose the topic of my education journey and how I struggled and overcame numerous obstacles, I acknowledge the resilience and determination I have to succeed and better understand my sovereignty over my academic and overall life. I realize that I have a lot more control over the outcome of my life instead of relying on luck. This challenges me to reflect on my past failures, mistakes, and negative moments, which is difficult, but I gain a better insight into who I am and my identity, and hearing other people’s stories influences my view of them and myself. I better understood who the presenter was, what we had in common, and what takeaways I could apply in my own life. Stories about overcoming struggles serve as motivation to improve and thrive; stories about fun and interesting experiences cheer us up; and stories about sorrowful and negative moments act as reminders to be grateful for what we have because we might be living in someone else’s dream. This experience makes me understand the power of story, both in our society and for Indigenous people. In The Marrow Thieves and Hunting By Stars, elders like Miigwans use stories to pass on and preserve their culture while keeping everyone strong, hopeful, and resilient, albeit living in a collapsing society. In our lives, stories educate, motivate, entertain, and reform our lives, while in the Indigenous lens, stories are a crucial way to preserve their culture and ensure it is passed onto future generations.
